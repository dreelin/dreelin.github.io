Formula Snapshot

For team i NO bet at price n_i and size q_i: r_{s,i} = -n_i if s = i, otherwise r_{s,i} = 1 - n_i W_s = W0 + sum_i q_i * r_{s,i} Incremental solve for team k (delta q >= 0): maximize pi_k*log(W_k - n_k*delta q) + sum_{s!=k} pi_s*log(W_s + (1-n_k)*delta q) Then apply your fraction f: delta_q_fractional = f * delta_q_full_kelly
